About

The Person Behind the Platform

I am Trivik Rao (Trivikrama Rao Padakandla), a Senior Salesforce Consultant and Solution Architect with more than 15 years of IT experience, including over 8 years specializing in Salesforce. Since August 2022, I have been operating independently through Trivik IT Services, delivering end-to-end Salesforce implementations for clients across Canada and the United States.
