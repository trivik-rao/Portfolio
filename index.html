About

I am Trivik Rao (Trivikrama Rao Padakandla), a Senior Salesforce Consultant with more than 15 years in IT and over 8 years specializing in Salesforce. Since 2022 I have operated independently through Trivik IT Services, delivering end-to-end Salesforce implementations for clients across Canada and the United States.

Before consulting, I built enterprise-scale foundations at Bell Canada, leading a Service Cloud and Amazon Connect CTI deployment serving 1,800+ users, and at Deloitte India across Salesforce Knowledge and large-scale case management implementations.

I own the full delivery lifecycle: technical discovery, requirements analysis, custom configuration and development, testing, and SFDX deployments to production.
