About

The Person Behind the Platform

I am Trivik Rao (Trivikrama Rao Padakandla), a Senior Salesforce Consultant and Specialist with more than 15 years of IT experience, including over 8 years specializing in Salesforce. Since August 2022, I have been operating independently through Trivik IT Services, delivering end-to-end Salesforce implementations for clients across Canada and the United States.

Before consulting, I built enterprise-scale foundations at Bell Canada, where I led a Salesforce Service Cloud and Amazon Connect CTI deployment serving over 1,800 users, and at Deloitte India, where I worked across Salesforce Knowledge and large-scale case management implementations.

I manage the full delivery lifecycle: technical discovery, requirements analysis, custom configuration and development, testing, and SFDX deployments to production.
