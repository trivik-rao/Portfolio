About

The Person Behind the Platform

I am Trivik Rao (Trivikrama Rao Padakandla), a Senior Salesforce Consultant with more than 15 years of IT experience, including over 8 years specializing in Salesforce. For the past 3 years, I have been operating as an independent contractor through my firm, Trivik IT Services. Before launching my consultancy, I built a strong foundation in the tech industry as a full time employee, working with major service based IT companies in India and later joining Bell Canada to deliver telecom focused enterprise solutions.

Throughout my career across corporate teams and independent consulting engagements, I have managed the full Salesforce delivery lifecycle. This includes handling technical discovery, analyzing business requirements, building custom configurations, testing, and managing SFDX deployments to production for clients across Canada and the United States.

Based in Halifax, Nova Scotia; available remotely for projects across Canada and the United States.
